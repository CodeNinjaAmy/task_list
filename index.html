How to Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

  • Get the jars of peanut butter and jelly, along with 2 slices of bread, a knife, and a spoon.
  • Place the slices of bread side by side on a plate.
  • Unscrew the lid to the peanut butter jar and use the knife to scoop peanut butter. Spread liberally on one side of one slice of bread. Add more peanut butter to taste.
  • Place the dirty knife aside, close the peanut butter jar, and put the jar away.
  • Unscrew the lid to the jar of jelly and use the spoon to scoop some out. Drop the jelly onto one side of the other slice of bread. Spread liberally with the back side of the spoon and add more jelly to taste.
  • Place the dirty spoon aside, close the jelly jar, and put the jar away.
  • Coming back to the slices of bread on your plate, put them together with the peanut butter and jelly facing each other on the inside of the sandwich.
  • Enjoy!
